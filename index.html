Accessibility

For those coming out of town, Kitchener Market has a paid parking garage that is accessible off of Cedar Street N. Otherwise, it is easily accessible by transit via the iON light rail and multiple bus lines. There is also bike racks available outside for those wishing to cycle to the event.
For taxis and ride share, we recommend using Waterloo Taxi 519-888-7777.


Overnight accomodations

If you need to stay the night somewhere, we recommend checking availability at the Crowne Plaza or The Walper, both just a few minutes walk from the Market. There are also several hotels in the north end, such as the Holiday Inn Waterloo, Courtyard Marriott Waterloo, or the Hampton Inn.
